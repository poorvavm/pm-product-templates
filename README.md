# pm-product-templates

A template library for healthcare software product management, covering the full product lifecycle from discovery through post-launch review.

## Templates

### Discovery & Strategy
- `01_Stakeholder_Interview_Notes.docx` — Capture stakeholder interviews: current state, pain points, and desired outcomes.
- `02_Product_Vision_Strategy.docx` — Document product vision, target users, OKRs, and healthcare compliance context.
- `03_Competitive_Analysis.docx` — Competitor feature matrix, SWOT analysis, and strategic recommendations.
- `04_Business_Case_Executive_Summary.docx` — Problem, proposed solution, ROI, risks, and go/no-go recommendation.

### Requirements & Planning
- `05_Product_Requirements_Document_PRD.docx` — Functional and non-functional requirements, including HIPAA and interoperability specs.
- `06_User_Story_Template.docx` — User story format with acceptance criteria, DoD, and PHI/FHIR impact notes.
- `07_HIPAA_Compliance_Checklist.docx` — PHI identification, technical/administrative/physical safeguards, and sign-off.

### Execution & Sprint Ceremonies
- `08_Sprint_Planning_Meeting_Notes.docx` — Sprint goal, team capacity, committed stories, and dependencies.
- `09_Daily_Standup_Notes.docx` — Daily team updates, blockers, and sprint health check.
- `10_Weekly_Status_Report.docx` — Weekly RAG status, accomplishments, risks, and decisions needed.
- `11_Sprint_Review_Demo_Notes.docx` — Demo outcomes, sprint metrics, stakeholder feedback, and backlog updates.
- `12_Sprint_Retrospective.docx` — Retrospective board, team health check, and commitments for the next sprint.

### Launch & Release
- `13_Go_to_Market_Plan.docx` — Target audience, messaging framework, launch checklist, and success metrics.
- `14_Release_Brief_Template.docx` — Release overview, what's changing, why it matters, and support channels.
- `15_UAT_Sign_off.docx` — Test scenarios, defects log, and formal UAT sign-off by PM, QA, clinical, and tech leads.

### Post-Launch
- `16_Post_Launch_Review.docx` — Goals vs. actuals, what worked, gaps, customer feedback, and next steps.
